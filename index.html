Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to all applications, games, web apps, and virtual reality games developed by huskyEstudioSoft, huskyEstudioGames, and huskyCodeAI.

Collection of Personal Data:

In our applications and games, we do not collect important personal information such as names, phone numbers, contacts, email addresses, or passwords. We value and respect the privacy of our users, and we do not request or store this type of sensitive information.

Third-Party Services:

In some of our applications, we may use third-party services to enhance the user experience.

Advertisements on Android and iOS:

In our Android and iOS applications, we utilize certain services that require an internet connection to display advertisements to our users. The following third-party services are used:

  • Only Games and mobile APPs: Unity Ads: You can find information about Unity Ads' privacy policy at the following link: http://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Chartboost: We recommend reviewing Chartboost's privacy policy at the following link: https://answers.chartboost.com/hc/en-us/articles/200780269-Privacy-Policy.
  • Applovin: For more information about Applovin's privacy policy, please visit: https://www.applovin.com/privacy.
  • Google Play services: If you wish to learn about the terms and privacy policies of Google Play services, you can refer to: https://developers.google.com/games/services/terms.

Unity 3D:

Regarding our games and virtual reality games, we use Unity Analytics. You can find more information about their privacy policy at: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Only bots APPS: Artificial intelligence bots & voice recognition apps: In the specific case of the AI bot, voice commands, and microphone accessibility usage, we also do not collect any personal information. We may use some third-party resources, such as the Microsoft Edge browser(Web Speech API) (https://privacy.microsoft.com/es-es/windows-10-microsoft-edge-and-privacy) or Google Speech API (https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en) and Azure Speech to text https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/legal/cognitive-services/language-service/data-privacy

In all our practices, we strive to protect the privacy and security of our users. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policies, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are committed to being transparent and providing the necessary support.
